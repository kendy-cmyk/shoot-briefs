Run Sheet
We're bringing two creators to your United Imaging PET CT launch night in Gosford. The night has two jobs: show the new machine to the referring doctors and oncologists in the room, and build social content that reaches patients across the Central Coast. One creator handles the polished track: photos, a promo, the event highlight and four sit-down interviews. The other grabs casual vox-pop and handheld b-roll for social. What's below is the short version. Tap any section for more, or use the tabs up top for the full shot list, questions and run sheet.
The shoot at a glance
ClientSpecialist Radiology
IndustryMedical imaging and radiology.
LocationSuite 13-14A/16 Watt St, Gosford NSW 2250
DateThursday 23 July 2026
FormatPro b-roll shot landscape. Interviews shot vertical for social, framed with safe margins so they crop cleanly to landscape if you ever need it. Promo and highlight delivered vertical, with a horizontal cut where it helps. UGC handheld and phone-style.
Patient privacy. This is the client's hard rule and a serious breach if we miss it. No patient names or details visible on any monitor, screen, worklist or paperwork, and no identifiable patients in frame without consent. Check every machine and reception shot before you press record.
